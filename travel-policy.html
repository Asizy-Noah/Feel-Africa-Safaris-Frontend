    Booking & Payment

    Booking Requirements

    • A completed booking form with all required information
    • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity from travel date
    • Confirmation of travel dates and number of participants

    Payment Terms

    • A non-refundable deposit of 30% is required to confirm your booking
    • The remaining 70% balance is due 30 days before departure
    • We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and mobile payment options
    • Late payments may result in cancellation of reservation

    Price Protection

    Prices are confirmed upon receipt of deposit. Exchange rate fluctuations after booking confirmation are not refundable.

    Cancellation Policy

    The following refund schedule applies to cancellations made in writing:

    Days Before Travel Refund Amount
    90+ days 100% refund minus booking fee
    60-89 days 80% refund
    30-59 days 50% refund
    15-29 days 25% refund
    Less than 15 days No refund

    Special Circumstances

    • Medical emergencies may qualify for exceptions. Documentation required.
    • Death in immediate family may qualify for rebooking without penalty
    • Travel restrictions due to government actions are subject to case-by-case review

    Modifications & Changes

    Itinerary Changes

    • Changes requested 60+ days before travel: Small administrative fee applies
    • Changes requested 30-59 days before travel: 10% change fee applies
    • Changes requested less than 30 days before travel: Subject to availability with applicable fees

    Date Changes

    Rebooking to different dates may incur additional costs based on season and availability. Premium season (July-September) changes may include surcharges.

    Travel Requirements

    Documentation

    • Valid passport with 6+ months validity beyond travel dates
    • Required visas for your country of origin
    • Travel insurance (highly recommended)
    • Yellow fever vaccination certificate (if required)

    Entry Permits

    Travelers are responsible for obtaining all necessary visas and permits. We provide guidance but cannot process applications on your behalf.

    Vaccinations

    Yellow fever vaccination is recommended. Consult your healthcare provider for other recommended vaccinations before travel to East Africa.

    Health & Safety

    Medical Fitness

    • Participants must be in good health to participate in safari activities
    • We recommend consulting your physician before travel
    • Pregnancy after 6 months is generally not recommended for safari travel
    • Inform us of any medical conditions when booking

    Safety Protocols

    • Follow all instructions from professional guides at all times
    • Do not approach, feed, or touch wildlife under any circumstances
    • Stay within safari vehicles during game drives
    • Respect wildlife and maintain safe distances as advised

    Travel Insurance

    We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical evacuation, trip cancellation, and personal belongings. Insurance is available at booking.

    Liability & Insurance

    Safari Adventures operates under East African tourism regulations. Our liability is limited to the cost of services provided. We are not responsible for:

    • Personal accidents, injuries, or illnesses
    • Loss, theft, or damage to personal belongings
    • Flight delays or cancellations (not under our control)
    • Actions or negligence of third-party service providers
    • Weather-related disruptions to activities
    • Natural disasters or unforeseeable circumstances

    Our Responsibility

    We maintain comprehensive liability insurance and follow strict safety protocols. Our professional guides are trained in first aid and emergency procedures.

    Force Majeure

    In cases of unforeseeable circumstances (natural disasters, pandemics, war, extreme weather), we reserve the right to:

    • Modify or cancel tours with full refund or alternative dates
    • Relocate activities to alternative locations
    • Postpone travel if conditions are unsafe

    COVID-19 & Health Restrictions

    If government restrictions prevent travel or require isolation, we offer full rebooking flexibility without penalties within 12 months of original travel date.
