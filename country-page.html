    Experience Uganda

    The Pearl of Africa.

    Uganda - Bigest Home to world's remaining Mountain gorillas

    If gorilla trekking safaris is on your wish list, then Uganda is a must visit destination to see gorillas in Africa on your safari tour, named ‘’Pearl of Africa’’ by Winston Churchill in his book (My African journey). Uganda is a home to more than half of the world’s remaining mountain gorillas, Uganda safaris combines Africa Big five safaris and primate safaris, making Uganda a unique African safari location with many things to do in a relatively small destination. Trekking mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest national park is a major highlight of numerous Uganda tour packages. Most safari itineraries in Uganda are tailored to include gorilla trekking; you can do a luxury gorilla trekking safari, budget gorilla trekking safari, and combined gorilla trekking with wildlife.
