    Frequently Asked Questions

    How far in advance should I book?

    We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for the best availability and rates, though we can sometimes accommodate last-minute bookings.

    What is your cancellation policy?

    See our Travel Policy page for detailed cancellation terms. Generally, cancellations made 60+ days before travel receive full refunds.

    Do you offer custom itineraries?

    Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized safari experiences tailored to your interests and budget.

    What is included in the package?

    Accommodation, meals, professional guides, park fees, and ground transportation are typically included. Ask about specific packages.

    Do you provide visa assistance?

    Yes, we provide guidance and documentation support for visa applications to all required countries.

    What about travel insurance?

    Travel insurance is highly recommended and we can provide recommendations for reputable providers.
